Electric GE90, the engine that makes you fly

If you have ever been on a hoover craft, you may recognize the design of our gigantic propeller driven engine in the rear of the car. With the Electric's aerodynamic shaped body and wings, we've managed to bring the old model's weaker hover into new soaring heights and potential!

Electric Rotax 912, the pod for improved handling

Getting off the ground is one thing, however, once your wheels are no longer on the ground, how do you then turn your car around? As you shift your gears over to "fly", the side pods of the Electric Rotax 912 are engaged, and synchronized with the motion sensors located in the front axis wheelhouses.

Pull open the doors and welcome the future!

We all remember the feeling when Christopher Loyd pulled open the driver seat door of the Delorean in the ’80s. Now you can impress with the same futuristic designed doors on your Electric. The doors open straight up towards you like a true nostalgic spacecraft rather than the sideways rotation of some of the more expensive-looking modern days sports cars.
